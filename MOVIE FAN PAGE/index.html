Character Bios

Rishab Shetty as Kaadubettu Shiva

Shiva, a passionate and rebellious young man deeply connected to his village's traditions and land. His character embodies the struggle against external forces threatening his community's heritage and way of life.

Sapthami gowda as Leela

Leela, who is integral to the village dynamics and Shiva's life. Her character adds emotional depth to the story, highlighting themes of love, loyalty, and the connection to tradition.

Kishore Kumar G as Murali

Murali, the role of a police officer, who is involved in the conflict surrounding the village's land issues. His character adds tension to the narrative, representing the law and authority that clash with the local traditions and beliefs.

Achyuth Kumar as Devendra Suttooru

Devendra Suttooru, the role of a village elder who serves as a guardian of tradition and culture in "Kantara." His character provides wisdom and guidance, emphasizing the importance of heritage and the community's connection to their land.

Pramod Shetty as Sudhakara

Sudhakara, the character of a village headman, who plays a significant role in navigating the conflicts between tradition and modernity. His character adds depth to the village dynamics, showcasing the complexities of leadership in the face of external challenges.
